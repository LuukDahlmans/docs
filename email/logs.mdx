## Overview
Email logs in WPShift provide a detailed record of all email activity on your domains. You can view logs for incoming and outgoing emails, including the sender, recipient, subject, and timestamp. Email logs help you monitor email activity, troubleshoot delivery issues, and ensure that your emails are being sent and received successfully.

### Viewing email logs
To view the email logs, click on the email address you want to view logs for in the Email tab. From the email address page, click on the **Logs** tab to access the email logs. Here you can see a list of all incoming and outgoing emails, along with details such as the sender, recipient, subject, and timestamp.
